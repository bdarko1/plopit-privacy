🚽 Plop It — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Plop It ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Duelr Ltd. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game Plop It.

Information We Collect

  • User ID: A randomly generated unique identifier stored on your device to save your progress and identify you in multiplayer matches.
  • Username: The display name you choose within the app.
  • Game data: Your scores, XP, rank, and match history, stored to power leaderboards and progression.
  • Purchase history: If you subscribe to Throne Pass, your subscription status is stored via RevenueCat to unlock features.

How We Use Your Information

  • To enable multiplayer gameplay and real-time leaderboards
  • To save your progress, XP, and rank across sessions
  • To manage your Throne Pass subscription and unlock features
  • To display your username and avatar in matches and leaderboards

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: We use Supabase to store game data and enable real-time multiplayer. Data is stored securely on Supabase servers.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase data in accordance with their privacy policy.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any sensitive personal information. We do not serve ads or share your data with advertisers.

Data Retention

Your game data is retained for as long as you use the app. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

Plop It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
